Where is Lesotho?

Lesotho is a little country of 30 355 km2 located within the territory of South Africa. This country, located in the mountains, has as its lowest point the confluence of the Orange and Makhaleng Rivers, at an altitude of 1,400 m, and as its highest point Mount Thabana Ntlenyana, at 3,482 m.

Who are the populations you are going to help?

These people, located in the Drakensberg region, are shepherds not on their own behalf and are between 12 and 35 years old. They live in different villages.

Why them?

These people already have access to school thanks to a volunteer from the village but it is very rudimentary. The volunteer teaches them the basics of English so that they can succeed in establishing a contract that is advantageous to them with their employer who owns the herds.

How can you help them?

You have the possibility of making donations that will be used to buy them supplies to help them learn English more effectively.You can also volunteer your time by becoming a teacher in their school. You can also buy the objects their produce by going to their village or by buying it here on the website.
